# **App Name**: HealthLink Connect

## Core Features:

- Secure Patient Authentication: Allows patients to register, log in, and securely access their personalized portal for managing their healthcare needs.
- Provider Directory: Enables patients to browse and search for available hospitals and doctors, view their profiles, and read about their specialties.
- AI Symptom Checker: An AI chatbot tool that guides patients through symptom assessment and provides preliminary recommendations or advises on seeking professional medical help.
- Virtual Appointment Booking: Facilitates the scheduling and management of virtual consultation appointments with chosen healthcare providers directly within the portal.
- Patient Dashboard: A personalized dashboard displaying upcoming appointments, a summary of recent interactions, and easily accessible health information.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: A deep, calming blue (#2E5299) symbolizing trust and professionalism in healthcare, providing strong contrast on light backgrounds.
- Background color: An ethereal, heavily desaturated blue (#ECF0F7) that offers a clean, airy, and calming foundation, supporting a 'blue and white' aesthetic.
- Accent color: A vibrant, analogous purplish-blue (#6845F5) to highlight key actions and calls-to-action, drawing the eye without being jarring.
- Headline and body font: 'Inter', a grotesque-style sans-serif, for its modern, clear, and highly readable qualities, ensuring legibility for all medical information.
- Clean, line-based, and universally recognizable medical and administrative icons to ensure clear communication and ease of navigation.
- A minimalist and intuitive layout featuring generous white space, clear information hierarchy, and responsive design for accessibility across devices. Information should be presented in digestible cards.
- Subtle, smooth transition animations for page navigation and component interactions, designed to provide a fluid and non-disruptive user experience.
